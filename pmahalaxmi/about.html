Piramal Mahalaxmi

A new life ahead

Situated in the Lower Parel vicinity of Mumbai, Mahalaxmi is a residential project by Piramal Realty. The project is spread over an area of 6.38 acres. It offers 2 and 3 BHK apartments with their size ranging from 755 sq ft to 887 sq ft. In terms of amenities, the project offers a closed car parking facility, fire safety and fire protection provision, internal roads & footpaths, landscaping & tree planting, sewerage system and storm water drains facility. One of the posh residential and commercial areas of Mumbai, Lower Parel is well-linked to different parts of the city via the Bridge, Annie Besant Road, Elphinstone Flyover and N M Joshi Marg. The Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport lies around 21 minutes away from this area. Commerce Senior Night College and the WLCI College are the educational institutions in this locality. The vicinity also entails many hospitals, shopping malls, cafes, restaurants, banks and ATMs.
